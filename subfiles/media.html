Photography:



This selection of portrait photographs is a showcase of my early DSLR camera work. As an assignment for UIUC’s MACS 150 (Digital Media Production) course, this collection was produced to give a glance into the character of my partner. Cool, calm, and observant, my partner is presented through the various shots, settings, and stagings strategically selected by myself.



Photoshop:


Another project made for UIUC’s MACS 150 (Digital Media Production) course, this TV poster is a showcase of my early Photoshop work. The assignment required producing a TV poster for a fake show starring myself, using a portrait photograph of myself, a set of external images repurposed and reproduced under fair use, and Photoshop’s many tools to paints a picture of what my show would be. Catch Robbie Sieczkowski’s latest HBO drama, User One: The VR Tales, tonight at 9!



Video:


A MACS 100 (Intro to Popular TV and Movies) production, this project is my directorial debut. Featuring the tremendous talent that UIUC’s intro MACS-level classes have to offer, this production was a fun project where I got to dip my toes in all areas of pre-production, production, and post-production of short narrative video content.
